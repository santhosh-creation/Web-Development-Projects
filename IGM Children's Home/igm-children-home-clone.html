About IGM Children's Home

Our Mission

IGM Children's Home is dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing environment for orphaned and vulnerable children. We believe every child deserves an opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive.

Through our comprehensive programs, we offer education, healthcare, emotional support, and life skills training to help children build a brighter future.

Located in Guduvancheri, Tamil Nadu, we have been serving the community for years, transforming lives and creating hope.
